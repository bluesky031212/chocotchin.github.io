Our Strawberry Protein Chocolate is a heavenly fusion of creamy premium cocoa and the refreshing sweetness of strawberries. Each bite delivers a smooth and satisfying experience, enriched with L-Theanine and Tryptophan — ingredients known to support relaxation and well-being. With no added sugar, it’s the perfect balance between indulgence and health.

Chocotchin Strawberry Protein is ideal as a quick pick-me-up, a guilt-free treat after training, or a thoughtful gift for health-conscious chocolate lovers. Packed with protein and crafted with care, it brings together delicious flavor, functional ingredients, and the pleasure of chocolate in every bite.
